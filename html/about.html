Overview

At AksuEasyride We're all about making motorcycle rides easy and safe. Our website's here to connect folks who love bikes with rides they can trust. We want to use cool tech to make booking a ride smooth and get you where you're going hassle-free.

Mission

Make bike rides simple and secure. We want to give riders a good time, making it easy to hop on a bike and have some fun while keeping things safe and chill.

Vision

Be the best motorcycle ride service everywhere. We want to set the bar high for great service and make sure bikers around the world can easily connect and enjoy the ride.

Goals & Objectives

  1. Make It Easy for staff and students
  2. To Keep It Safe.
  3. To Go Everywhere.
  4. To Care for the Planet.
  5. Create a fun online space where riders can connect, share stories, and have a good time..
  6. To establish a strong reputation as a trusted and reliable service provider for undergraduate students and staffs.

Core Values

  • Keep It Safe: Always make sure our riders are safe and sound.
  • Try New Things: Use cool technology and keep getting better.
  • Be Dependable: Make sure you can always count on us for a ride.
  • Stick Together: Build a community where everyone feels at home.
  • Help the Earth: Do our part to take care of the planet.
